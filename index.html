DSWD Tara Basa program (BukSU collaboration)

University students serve as Tutors and Youth Development Workers in designated communities. This program is designed to build capacity and provide tutoring and youth development activities. If you’re a student or alumni, you can inquire at BukSU’s Extension Unit or the DSWD Bukidnon office for application details.
